Campus Resonance

An interactive translation of CSUSM hydration station data into sound. Each building becomes a clickable point of resonance based on its bottle-saving total.

Key

Each button represents one CSUSM building with a hydration station. When selected, the building’s data appears below and its recorded vial tone can be played. Differences in tone reflect differences in the dataset. Together, the webpage acts as an interactive sound-based translation of campus sustainability impact.
