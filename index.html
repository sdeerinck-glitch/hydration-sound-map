Campus Resonance

Campus Resonance is an interactive translation of CSUSM hydration station data. Each building can be selected to reveal its bottle-saving total and associated sound, allowing the data to be experienced interactively.

Key
Each box represents a CSUSM building from the Hydration Station dataset. Each tone is created by blowing across a vial filled according to the data: buildings with higher bottle-saving totals are represented by fuller vials and higher-pitched tones, while lower totals are represented by less full vials and lower-pitched tones.
Source: “Hydration Stations | Sustainability | CSUSM”
