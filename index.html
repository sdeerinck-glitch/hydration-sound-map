Campus Resonance

An interactive translation of CSUSM hydration station data into sound. Each building becomes a clickable point of resonance based on its bottle-saving total.

Key

Each button represents one CSUSM building with a hydration station. Each building will be paired with a recorded vial tone. The sound for each vial is based on a liquid level assigned from that building’s bottle-saving total. Differences in tone reflect differences in the data. Together, the webpage acts as an interactive sound-based translation of campus sustainability impact.

Audio recordings will appear here once added.
